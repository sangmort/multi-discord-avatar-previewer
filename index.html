Discord Avatar Previewer

Visualize multiple images as discord avatars

How to Use

Click "upload" button & select up to 10 images. These images will be styled the way they are in the discord app, giving you a preview of what it would look like when you use it on discord. To remove an individual preview, click on it. To remove multiples, hit the reset button to clear all the previews.
